Tag Request Form

  1. Ensure this form is downloaded and opened directly in browser, not a file previewer.
  2. It is recommened to send tags in batches of at most 100 in separate emails to reduce lag.
  3. Review the TagsRUs standard and ensure all tags are in compliance. Standards document available upon request.
  4. Select your mill from the dropdown at the top.
  5. Ensure you have the right columns in order with proper headers, then paste your formatted tag information from Excel into the text area or manually fill in the table.
    If you are processing a lot of tags, be sure your formatting matches the TableName through Max headers and is spelled like the available units/data types/transaction frequency dropdown options.
    Ask for help if needed.
  6. It is STRONGLY preferred to use an IP for the PLC address to allow the ROC to quickly and accurately find the right tag.
  7. Ensure tags exist and are ready to record data so the ROC can validate functionality
    Excessive use of placeholders is discouraged
  8. Click "Process Data" to parse your table.
  9. Review each row for accuracy, fix any validation errors.
  10. Click "Copy Table" and then "Submit Data" when finished.
  11. Paste your table into the email and send.
Please select a mill before proceeding.
